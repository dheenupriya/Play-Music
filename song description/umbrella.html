About The Song:


"Play Date" (sometimes stylized as "Playdate") is the fourteenth track by Melanie Martinez featured on the deluxe version of her debut album, Cry Baby. It is the first deluxe track on the album, the second being Teddy Bear and the third being Cake. On June 26, 2020, Play Date was included as the B-side to Fire Drill on Spotify. On May 26, 2020, Melanie released an lyric video for the song due to it's rise in popularity, originating from TikTok. She previously stated that she was working on a homemade music video during the COVID-19 pandemic, but later decided to cancel the project and focus on her extended play, After School. On October 25, 2020 the demo of the song was leaked. It had previously been teased on the After-School All-Stars TikTok livestream.
