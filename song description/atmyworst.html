About The Song:


“At My Worst” was released at the end of January and it is already putting many people in their feels. The simple yet catchy song talks about wanting to find someone who will love you no matter what. The song opens with a slow guitar intro accompanied with the lyrics, “Can I call you baby?/ Can you be my friend?/ Can you be my lover until the very end?” This tune has a sweet, innocent feel that will make anyone’s heart swoon way past the month of February. The melody of the chords comforts while the lyrics say the things that the reader may be afraid to say out loud. Love songs aren’t something new for Pink Sweat$..
