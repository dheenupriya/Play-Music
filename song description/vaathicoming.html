About The Song:


Vaathi Coming Song Lyrics or Vathi Coming Song is the second single from “Master” tamil film starring Thalapathy “Vijay” in a lead role. This song is sung by “Anirudh Ravichander & Gana Balachandar” and music composed by “Anirudh Ravichander“. Lyrics works are penned by “Gana Balachandar”.
